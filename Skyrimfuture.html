The Future

This is not just a look at the future of Skyrim, but the future of the elder scrolls series as a whole

The Elder Scrolls 6 is in development and is said to be a "next gen" Elder Scrolls game.

The community has seen what a next gen Elder Scrolls game could look like thanks to several skyrim mods and The Elder Scrolls Oblivion Remastered released in 2025.
