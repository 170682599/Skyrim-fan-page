Gameplay

This will go over the gameplay of skyrim

At the start of the game you get to chose your class, abilities, and what you look like.

You can either fight enemies with weapons that you find in caves or dungeons or with magic, you can unlock diferent spells throughout the game to use either for battle or healing. You can also unlock shouts throughout the game by killing dragons.
