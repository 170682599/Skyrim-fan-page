Skyrim's History

This is the history of Skyrim

Skyrim began development just after the release of Fallout 3 in 2008.

The Elder Scrolls series was already successful because of Morrowind winning game of the year and RPG of the year in 2002 and Oblivion winning game of the year and best role playing game of the year.

skyrim released on the eleventh of november 2011, or 11.11.11. It was originally released for pc, ps3 and xbox 360 but was later re released with numerous editions, one of which being the legendary edition releaased on pc and modern consoles, others being the vr edition in 2017 and the anniversary edition in 2021

Skyrim's rich gameplay, replayability, story and extensive mod support are the reasons the game is still played today, all these years later.
